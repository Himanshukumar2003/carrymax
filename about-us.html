About us

Welcome to Carrymax Lifts

We CARRYMAX LIFTS proudly introduce ourselves as a Quality Centric Company engaged in Manufacturing, Supply, Installation, Testing, Commissioning, Repair, Modernization & Maintenance of Lifts of all types, be it Passenger, Hospital, Automobile, Goods or Dumbwaiter for Commercial, Industrial or Residential premises.

At the initial stages we were confined to Erection & Commissioning Contractor for renowned manufacturers of Elevator Industry. As we had envisaged a long term vision, all marketing activities were done under the brand 'CARRYMAX' which fact has really paved the way for establishing a strong and brand-conscious organization in the days to follow.

We are one of the fastest growing Elevator Company having own manufacturing facility setup in IMT of Industrial Town Faridabad (Haryana). Our products are manufactured using high grade material and we are adequately equipped with latest manufacturing and technological infrastructure to ensure the reliability of the equipment installed by us.
